Ways You Can Help

There are many organizations running in NYC that aim to help the homeless and the best way to help is by volunteering or donating to their caus to reach out to more people in need. There are more homeless people than the organizations and charities can distribute to, so donating directly to the homeless in the parks or streets of NYC is always encouraged!

It doesn't matter how much you can donate or contribute; any help is welcomed!

You can donate:

  • loose change
  • used socks
  • canned food
  • bottles of water

Spreading Awareness

There have been protests and news regarding anti-homeless architecture that aim to prohibit people from sleeping in public spaces, but this only adds onto to the problem as those who can't go to a shelter will have no where safe to rest. It's highly encouraged to spread awareness of these government and private companies' actions against the people of NYC!
